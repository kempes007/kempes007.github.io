NIKE OLYMPICS EXPERIENTIAL CONCEPT

For a pitch for the Nike Olympics we were asked to create a campaign to grow expectations for the Olympics and make people feel like athletes. I designed the visuals for the campaign that my team and I created with the concept “Raise the bar”. The idea was that each individual would send their km run to a visual infographic that was placed around the city and Nike stores. The total km run was shown in a visually attractive way to show how each one of them was contributing to raise the bar. The experiential activation was interactive and users were able to see their personal profiles and the amount of km run.
