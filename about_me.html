ABOUT ME

Making the world a nicer place

Victoria has over 10 years experience as a digital designer & digital art director working internationally across Spain, Australia & England. She has worked in some of the best agencies like Grey group (Barcelona), Herraiz&Soto (Barcelona), DDB (Sydney) and R/GA (Sydney), having won several awards with her work. Some of the clients she has worked with include Nike, Google, Toyota, Samsung, IKEA, Camper, SEAT. With a strong conceptual thinking, she likes to push the boundaries of design, achieving excellence and making each client feel unique.
