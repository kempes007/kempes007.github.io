ABOUT ME

CREATING A NEW DESIGN SYSTEM FOR TOYOTA AUSTRALIA

I supported the opening of R/GA Melbourne and the development of R/GA Australia’s largest systematic account. This was achieved by partnering with UX to build client relationships, defining a new brand strategy, prioritizing projects to achieve the required future vision and mentoring an embedded design team. As a design lead, the objective was to create a simple yet beautiful interface where the power lied on details. All the parts of the interface breathed the Toyota brand, even in the interactions and transitions. Below are some screens of different sections of Toyota.com.au where the new design look and feel was applied to. Firstly, my team and I redesigned the editorial on-line magazine with a clean and editorial look and feel. The readability of these pages was the most important thing to consider, that’s why I decided to use a Sans Serif font. I also used a clear grid to dispose of the elements with a clear visual hierarchy.

For users to be able to find a dealer we created a very clear and an easy layout. Using the functionality of Google Maps as a UX inspiration, I prioritized the location of the user so the nearest dealers would come up. The design look and feel had to be clean so that the information would be visually clear and accessible. See the screens below.

Another example of how I succeeded in simpliflying all the functionalities was my contribution into turning a very complicated functionality, such as a finance calculator, into a visually attractive interface. In this case, we had to overcome multiple challenges, such as the numerous legal limitations surrounding the project, the requirement of all numbers having to be the same font size and having to include a lot of disclaimers, while still making it very easy and appealing to the user.
