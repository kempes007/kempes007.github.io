AN EXPERIENTIAL WEATHER APP FOR CAMPER

Camper understands optimism through half imagination and half craft. To communicate this concept we created an experiential weather forecast app where the weather elements were made of prÃªt-a-porter materials and turned them into musical instruments that react to touch.


THE ASK

I was asked to create concepts for several weather elements. I had to decide the character and behaviour of each element, the materials used and the interactions. I created and concepted several weather elements through mockups as detailed as possible so that the 3D designer would build them as beautifully as possible.


THE STORM

I decided the storm had to have a rocker look and feel. Therefore I used leather and zips for all the elements. The idea of the interactions was to unzip the bolts which therefore would have a rocking sound. The interaction would be from top to bottom, very similar to how rockers play their guitar.
